Training Curriculum

Our comprehensive WallStreetPrep curriculum covers all essential financial modeling skills for investment analysis.

Module 1: Financial Statement Analysis

Master the fundamentals of financial statement analysis with a focus on interpreting income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements for investment decisions.

Income Statement Analysis

Revenue recognition, COGS, operating expenses, and profitability metrics

Balance Sheet Analysis

Asset valuation, liability assessment, and equity analysis

Cash Flow Analysis

Operating, investing, and financing cash flows assessment

Ratio Analysis

Liquidity, profitability, solvency, and efficiency ratios

Module 2: Financial Modeling Fundamentals

Build robust financial models from scratch using industry best practices and Excel efficiency techniques.

Model Structure Design

Best practices for building scalable, flexible models

Modeling Best Practices

Error-proofing, documentation, and auditing techniques

Excel Efficiency

Advanced functions, shortcuts, and productivity tools

Circular References

Managing circularities in financial models

Module 3: DCF Modeling & Valuation

Master discounted cash flow valuation techniques to determine intrinsic company value and make informed investment decisions.

Free Cash Flow Calculation

Unlevered vs. levered free cash flow methodologies

Forecasting Techniques

Top-down vs. bottom-up revenue forecasting approaches

WACC Calculation

Cost of equity, cost of debt, and optimal capital structure

Terminal Value Methods

Perpetuity growth vs. exit multiple approaches

Module 4: Advanced Modeling Techniques

Develop sophisticated models for complex financial scenarios and specialized industry applications.

M&A Modeling

Accretion/dilution analysis and merger consequences

LBO Modeling

Leveraged buyout analysis and returns calculation

Real Estate Modeling

Development pro formas and stabilized cash flow analysis

Scenario & Sensitivity Analysis

Building flexible models for different economic scenarios
